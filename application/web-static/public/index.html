About Me

Hi There! :-)

Professional Life

I'm just your friendly neighborhood software architect. With over 20 years of delivering high quality enterprise applications at Fortune 500 companies. I've worn a few hats in my time. From full-stack coding to leading high-performing tech teams to managing projects, people, and budgets to production support to tackling hairy performance problems, to architecting a number of large-scale distributed, resilient applications. I've worked in a number of industries. And I've been fortunate to work with a lot of smart, talented people...and have fun along the way.

Life Outside of Work

In my real job, I'm a dad helping to raise my 3 awesome kids and living life. I enjoy nerding out to the incredible craft and artistry of golden age hip-hop. And engaging people of all backgrounds and walks of life.One of my greatest joys has been watching my kids grow and develop their personalities and find their talents. Helping to guide them as they navigate life...and teenage years - yikes! It's been fun watching them grow up fully in the digital age... "wait, Dad...you mean you had to get in a car and drive all the way out to a store when you wanted to hear new music? Tell us more about the stone age."
